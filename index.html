BODYWEIGHT

Bodyweight classes are for all ability levels. Classes will use predominantly bodyweight exercise to provide a well-rounded workout addressing strength and mobility requirements to develop muscular skeletal health and performance. You will be carefully progressed towards increasingly challenging movements and exercises.

MOBILITY

Mobility class will focus on flexibility, stability and joint preparation. This is the perfect class to compliment your training. Addressing mobility restrictions such as tight shoulders, hips and weakness at end ranges of flexibility. This class is a must to really focus on increasing your resilience and allowing you to train harder pain free.
